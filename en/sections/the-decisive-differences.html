⚠️ The decisive differences

Cosmic world-axes, humanistic rankings, and Plato and the Buddha.

Dansk See this section inside full text Back to category

Here their paths diverge radically:
🧘 Buddha:

• No soul

• No eternal identity

• No cosmic reward

• Exit from the whole cosmos

• Liberation open to all

🧠 Plato:

• Eternal soul

• Individual identity remains

• Dwelling in the world of forms

• Cosmos remains

• Liberation especially for philosophers

In short:

➡️ The Buddha wants out of the cosmos

➡️ Plato wants upward in the cosmos
