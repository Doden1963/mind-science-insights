🌌 Big Bounce cosmology and MN 4 (Bhayabherava Sutta)

World cycles, rebirth, heavens, mahākappa, and conclusion.

DanskSee this section inside full textBack to category

Source:
https://suttacentral.net/mn4/en/bodhi?lang=en&reference=none&highlight=false
“Once my concentrated mind was purified, bright, unblemished, rid of defilement, pliant, workable, steady, and imperturbable, I directed it to the knowledge of recollecting past lives. I recollected my many past lives … many aeons of contraction of the world, many aeons of expansion of the world, many aeons in which these two movements followed one another in inconceivably long cosmic cycles …
… With the divine eye … I saw beings passing away and reappearing … and I understood how beings wander on according to their actions …
… I directly knew … ‘This is suffering.’ … ‘This is the path leading to the end of suffering.’ …
… ‘Birth is ended, the holy life has been lived, what had to be done has been done, there is no more returning to any state of being.’ …
… It is because I see two benefits that I still seek secluded resting places … I see a pleasant abiding for myself here and now, and I have compassion for future generations.”
