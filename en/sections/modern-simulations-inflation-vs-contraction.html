🧪 Modern simulations: inflation vs. contraction

World cycles, rebirth, heavens, mahākappa, and conclusion.

DanskSee this section inside full textBack to category

Scientific simulations show:
• Inflation often creates inhomogeneous universes.
• Contraction makes the universe more homogeneous, flat, and isotropic.
This harmonizes surprisingly well with Buddhism’s teaching that after a contraction, universes unfold from a subtle, harmonious state.
