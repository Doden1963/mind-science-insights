🌌 Humanistic assessment

World-axes, humanistic rankings, and Plato and the Buddha.

DanskSee this section inside full textBack to category

Both are extremely humanistic – but in different ways.
The Buddha:
• Universal access
• No elite
• No god
• No hierarchy
• The ending of suffering as goal
➡️ The most radically humanistic doctrine of liberation in antiquity.
Plato:
• Reason above gods
• Ethics without revelation
• The dignity of the soul is central
But:
• Hierarchical
• Elitist
• The soul remains bound to the cosmos
➡️ Very humanistic philosophically – but not cosmically radical.
