🥉 Daoist tradition – Mount Kunlun

Cosmic world-axes, humanistic rankings, and Plato and the Buddha.

Dansk See this section inside full text Back to category

Humanism: medium-high
Kunlun functions as a cosmic center and dwelling place of the immortals.

• No creator god

• No theology of punishment

• Harmony and non-violence at the center

But the focus is more individual than universal, and there is no systematic ethics for relieving everyone’s suffering.
