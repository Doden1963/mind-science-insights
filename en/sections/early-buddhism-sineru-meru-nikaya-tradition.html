🥇 Early Buddhism – Sineru / Meru (Nikāya tradition)

Cosmic world-axes, humanistic rankings, and Plato and the Buddha.

Dansk See this section inside full text Back to category

Humanism: very high

• No creator god

• No eternal soul

• No absolute hierarchy

• All beings can reach liberation

• Even heavenly realms are impermanent

The cosmos is not described to explain power, but to show that only Nibbāna is an exit from the whole cycle.
This is the most consistently humanistic cosmology in antiquity.
