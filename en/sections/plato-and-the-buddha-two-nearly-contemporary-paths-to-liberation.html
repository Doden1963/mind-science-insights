🌌 Plato and the Buddha – two nearly contemporary paths to liberation

World-axes, humanistic rankings, and Plato and the Buddha.

DanskSee this section inside full textBack to category

Around 400 BCE, two of the greatest thinkers in world history appear almost simultaneously:
• the Buddha in India
• Plato in Greece
They knew nothing of each other, yet independently developed some of the deepest theories about:
• suffering
• knowledge
• soul / consciousness
• liberation
• cosmic order
A comparison between them shows both striking similarities and decisive differences.
