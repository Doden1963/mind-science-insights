✝️ Jesus, “many dwellings,” and a possible counterpart to Nirvana

World cycles, rebirth, heavens, mahākappa, and conclusion.

DanskSee this section inside full textBack to category

I do not think it can be ruled out that there is a counterpart to Nirvana spoken by Jesus in the Gospel of Thomas — before organized religion defined it as impossible for even a single being in all galaxies and universes at all times to reach Jesus’ level in relation to God.
Gospel of Thomas (logion 18):
The disciples said to Jesus: Tell us how our end will be. Jesus said: Have you then found the beginning, that you are looking for the end? For where the beginning is, there will the end be. Blessed is the one who takes his place at the beginning; he shall know the end and shall not taste death.
https://vincentius.dk/Thomas-evangeliet.pdf
Jesus also said according to the Gospel of John:
“Do not let your hearts be troubled! Believe in God and believe in me! In my Father’s house are many dwelling places; if not, would I have told you that I go to prepare a place for you?”
Just as there are many “dwellings” in the Father’s house, Buddhism also describes many different forms of existence in other planes of existence.
