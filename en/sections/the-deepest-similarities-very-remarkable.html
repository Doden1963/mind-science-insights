🌟 The deepest similarities (very remarkable)

Cosmic world-axes, humanistic rankings, and Plato and the Buddha.

Dansk See this section inside full text Back to category

Despite major differences, they share some almost identical insights:

• The sensory world is not ultimate reality

• Ignorance binds the human being

• Knowledge is liberating

• A higher level of consciousness is possible

• Ethical life is necessary for knowledge

Plato:
“The one who has seen the good will no longer commit injustice.”
The Buddha:
“Ignorance is the root of all suffering.”
In practice, both describe:

• the ascent of consciousness

• purification of the mind

• liberation through insight
