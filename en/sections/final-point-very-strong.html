🔑 Final point (very strong)

Cosmic world-axes, humanistic rankings, and Plato and the Buddha.

Dansk See this section inside full text Back to category

It is almost unimaginable that:

• two thinkers

• at opposite ends of the world

• without contact

• at the same time

both arrive at the idea:

👉 that the human being can be liberated through knowledge

👉 that the sensory world is not ultimate reality

👉 that ethics and insight are inseparably connected

But where Plato still preserves:

• the soul

• the hierarchy

• the cosmos

the Buddha goes one step further:

👉 he dissolves the very soul

👉 abolishes the whole cosmos

👉 and offers a universal exit from all existence

That makes the Buddha the most radically humanistic cosmic thinker in antiquity –
and Plato the most humanistic philosopher in the West.
